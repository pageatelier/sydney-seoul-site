BRAND STORY

Sydney to Seoul

A sensibility born in Sydney,
completed in Seoul

Sydney Seoul is a contemporary dining experience that brings together
the experiences and inspirations of two very different cities onto one table.

Built on the free-spirited sensibility and respect for ingredients
we learned in Sydney, and the delicate taste and seasonal rhythm
that define Seoul, we look at familiar flavors in a new light.

Crossing the boundaries between two cities,
we present dishes completed in Sydney Seoul's own balance and style.

CHEF

The Chef

Minhu Kim Owner Chef

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Australia, the chef developed
his own sensibility through Australia's free-spirited food culture
and its diverse range of ingredients.

In 2020, he opened 'Up To Me', an Australian-style casual modern
dining restaurant offering both vegan dishes and a regular menu,
and in 2025 he introduced 'Woogome', a fusion restaurant centered
on Korean beef that brings together French and Korean cuisine.

Moving freely across different cultures and cooking styles,
he expresses the true flavor of each ingredient
in a refined and inventive way.

PHILOSOPHY

Our Philosophy

We bring the place and season
an ingredient was born into onto one plate

We believe good cooking begins with
a deep understanding of, and respect for, our ingredients.

We carefully consider home-grown seasonal vegetables and
ingredients sourced from nature in Australia and Korea —
Australian beef, kangaroo, barramundi, and more —
expressing them in ways that let their flavor and texture shine through.

By adding a fresh perspective and delicate technique to familiar ingredients,
we create dishes uniquely Sydney Seoul's own,
where the landscapes and seasons of two regions come into harmony.
